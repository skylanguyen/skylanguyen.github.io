Hong Kong Style Mahjong

Hong Kong Style Mahjong is a good place to start for total beginners. It is probably the easiest ruleset to understand and basically all eastern variants of mahjong will at least feel familiar to you if you understand Hong Kong Style.

Riichi (Japanese) Mahjong

Riichi is more of a defensive style of Mahjong that focuses more on closed hands and careful discards. It also utilizes bonus tiles (Dora) to boost your hand's points and requires stricter focus on the game.
