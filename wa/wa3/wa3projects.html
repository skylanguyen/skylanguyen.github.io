Typographic Lyrics

For this project, I explored playing with font sizes, weights, and other features to illustrate the lyrics and mood of a chosen song. I tried to capture the same emotion while also limiting the intense use of graphic elements.

I redesigned the poster mailer for the Colorado Symphony. I played with different color palettes, font combinations, negative space, and other design fundamentals in order to print it in real time.
